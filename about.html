About This Project

Learn more about the database and its purpose.

Our Mission

We aim to provide a comprehensive plant and tree database that is accessible to everyone, including students, gardeners, and hobbyists. Every entry includes scientific details, habitat, lifecycle, identification features, and cultural or historical notes.

How It Works

The database is powered by a JSON dataset with detailed information on plants, trees, shrubs, fungi, mosses, and lichens. You can search by common name, scientific name, habitat, or lifecycle to quickly find the data you need.

Contributors

This project is maintained and updated by a team of botanists and plant enthusiasts, ensuring accuracy and reliability of all entries.

Contact Us

If you have questions, suggestions, or want to contribute, please use the Contact page to reach out. We welcome community involvement!
